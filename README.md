# The State of Mobile Money in Kenya

An editorial data story examining Kenya's mobile money ecosystem; 18 years of growth, market structure, regional adoption gaps, and shifting use cases. Five interactive charts, data tables, and evidence-based policy recommendations.

---

## Overview

Kenya's M-Pesa is one of the most successful fintech products in history. This report explores how mobile money grew from a simple SMS transfer tool into the backbone of Kenya's entire economy — and what the data reveals about what comes next.

> Open `Mobile_Money_Kenya_Report.html` in any browser. No installation required.

## Key Findings

- Transaction values grew **514%** over 10 years — from KES 2.8T (2015) to KES 17.2T (2024)
- Merchant payments grew from **11% to 28%** of transaction volume in just 5 years
- The gender gap is closing — female users rose from **32% to 46%** since 2015
- North Eastern Kenya remains severely underserved at **38% penetration**
- M-Pesa holds **96.1% market share** — one of the highest concentrations globally
